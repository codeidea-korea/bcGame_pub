Wild Coaster (PG Soft): GAME Review & Theme

With the arrival of summer, the enticing warmth beckons everyone to venture out and savor some thrills. Tourists from across the globe, as well as locals seeking excitement, flock to amusement parks.

Shockventures amusement park is renowned for its exhilarating rides and delectable cuisine. This year, the park is abuzz with visitors eager to experience its latest and most electrifying attraction, the "Wild Coaster." This ride boasts multiple heart-pounding 360-degree turns and an adrenaline-pumping 90-degree drop that leaves riders screaming with joy. Do you dare to take on the Wild Coaster?

In the world of gaming, "Wild Coaster" is a 6-reel, 4-row video slot developed by PG Soft, featuring an additional reel at the top and bottom of reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. It introduces expanding Wild symbols, a captivating Free spin feature, and the chance to unlock win multipliers with just 4 Scatter symbols. Furthermore, each additional Scatter symbol triggers an extra 2 free spins, potentially leading to astonishing payouts of up to 50,000 times your initial stake. Will you take a ride on this wild gaming adventure?

About Wild Coaster at BC.GAME

At BC.GAME, the Wild Coaster boasts a unique setup featuring six reels and four rows, accompanied by an additional reel positioned above and below reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. Keep a keen eye out for the expanding Wild symbols that can stretch across the reels, significantly enhancing your chances of landing winning combinations and injecting a dose of excitement into the gameplay. The game offers a flexible range of paylines, starting at 4,096 and expanding up to an impressive 20,736.

What truly raises the stakes is the opportunity to snag a maximum win of 50,000 times your initial stake, adding an exhilarating dimension to every spin as you chase substantial rewards.

Wild Coaster strikes a harmonious balance with its medium volatility and a remarkable RTP of 97%, ensuring a gameplay experience that combines consistent thrills with the potential for lucrative wins. The hit rate stands at 32.07%.

Crafted to accommodate players using both iOS and Android devices, this slot ensures a smooth and hassle-free mobile gaming experience, allowing you to engage in this fruit-filled adventure effortlessly from your smartphone, regardless of your location.

Pay Table and Bet Size

At BC.GAME, the Wild Coaster slot, the grid adopts a 6x4 layout, providing you with the potential for up to 20,736 paylines. Betting flexibility ranges from a minimum of $0.2 to a maximum of $100 per spin. The game offers the thrilling possibility of a maximum payout of 50,000 times your initial wager.

Inside the Wild Coaster slot, you'll encounter a diverse array of 13 distinct symbols, each playing a pivotal role in the gameplay. The 10, J, and Q symbols yield payouts ranging from 1x to 4x your bet. The K and A symbols offer rewards from 4x to 10x. The Beverage symbol provides payouts spanning from 6x to 15x your bet. The Hot Dog symbol grants payouts of 6x to 15x. The Booth symbol offers returns of 8x to 30x. The Tent symbol delivers payouts ranging from 10x to 60x. The Ferris wheel symbol pays out from 20x to 70x. The Roller Coaster symbol offers rewards spanning from 30x to 80x. Additionally, the game features both Wild and Scatter symbols.

In this dynamic slot, Cascading Wins keeps the game exciting, replacing winning symbols with fresh ones falling from above. The Win Multiplier starts at x1, climbing by +1 with each cascading win. Stacked Wilds on the top and bottom reels contain 2 or 3 wilds, disappearing one by one with each win. When they appear in the same position on both reels, all symbols in between turn wild, too. The Bonus Round, triggered by 4+ consecutive scatters, awards 10 free spins with an increasing win multiplier. Wilds stay until they help a win or the round ends. Non-UK players and eligible participants can buy the bonus for 75x the stake, guaranteeing at least 4 triggering scatters on the next spin.

Wild Symbols and Expanding Symbols

When Wild symbols land on the upper and lower reels, they come stacked with 2 or 3 Wilds on top of each other. The uppermost Wild in the stack is removed when it contributes to a win, and this process continues until all stacked Wilds are utilized. The last remaining Wild is then eliminated, along with the other symbols that formed a winning combination. In cases where Wilds appear in identical positions on both the upper and lower reels, all symbols in between also transform into Wilds.

Free Spins and Scatter Symbols

The Free Spins feature is activated when you land 4 or more scatters anywhere in view within the same cascading sequence, granting you 10 free spins. Additionally, you receive an extra 2 spins for each additional triggering scatter beyond the initial 4. The win multiplier increments by +1 with each cascading win and remains in effect throughout the entire bonus round without resetting. Wild symbols stay in position until they contribute to a win or until the bonus round concludes.

Cascading Reels

The Cascading Wins feature removes symbols that result in wins, and new or existing symbols then fall from above to fill the gaps. Initially, the Win Multiplier begins at 1x during each base game spin, and it grows by +1 with every successive cascading win.

Feature Buy-in

For those interested, you have the option to acquire the bonus round through the Bonus Buy Menu. This will require an investment of 75 times your stake, ensuring you receive a minimum of 4 triggering scatters on the subsequent spin.

Summary

Wild Coaster by PG Soft, available at BC.GAME, offers an exhilarating and dynamic slot experience with its innovative features. The game's arrangement, comprising six reels and four rows, with an extra reel above and below reels 2, 3, 4, and 5, offers an astounding 20,736 potential paylines. With the potential for a maximum win of 50,000 times your wager, Wild Coaster keeps players on the edge of their seats. The Cascading Wins mechanic, expanding Wild symbols, and increasing Win Multiplier create an action-packed gameplay atmosphere. The Bonus Round further intensifies the excitement, providing free spins with a growing win multiplier and locked Wilds. Additionally, the option to purchase the bonus round adds a unique element to the game. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to slots, Wild Coaster promises an engaging and thrilling ride through its well-designed layout. Give it a spin and brace yourself for a wild adventure.
